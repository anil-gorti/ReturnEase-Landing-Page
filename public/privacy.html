1. Introduction

[Dummy Company Name] Private Limited ("we," "our," or "us") operates ReturnEase. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information to facilitate your move to India.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name and email address.
  • Relocation Data: Current country of residence, target city in India, family size, school requirements, and move timeline.
  • Financial Context: High-level details regarding US/Global assets (e.g., "RSU holder") to provide relevant RNOR tax frameworks. We do not collect bank account passwords or social security numbers.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction patterns on our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your Personalized Return Playbook.
  • To provide city-specific school and commute recommendations.
  • To match you with vetted vendors (only with your explicit consent).
  • To communicate updates regarding Indian regulatory changes (FEMA, Aadhaar, Tax).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Sale of Data: We never sell your personal information to brokers or marketing agencies.
  • Vetted Vendors: We share your contact details with our partner CAs, logistics firms, or real estate advisors only if you explicitly request an introduction.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by Indian law or governmental requests.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Access to user relocation data is limited to authorized ReturnEase consultants only.
  • We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, but no electronic system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt-Out: You may unsubscribe from our relocation updates at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the permanent deletion of your relocation profile and Notion workspace by emailing us.
  • Access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about your move.

7. Data Retention

We retain your relocation data for as long as your account is active or for up to 24 months following your "Target Move Date" to assist with post-landing support (e.g., Phase 7 Integration). After this, data is anonymized for analytical purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

While we assist with "Schooling Strategy," we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All data regarding minors (e.g., grade level) must be provided by the parent or legal guardian.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in Indian data privacy laws (DPDP Act). We will notify you of material changes via email.
